Key Features

Omnichannel Consumer Engagement

Sponsors, Teams, Influencers, and Marketers can engage any audience by any form - by Text, Email, Mobile, Social, Web, Banner Ads, QR Code, Print and Digital Signage.

Serialized & One-Time Use

Because offers are individually serialized and tied to an associated one-time-use payment code, offer budgets are controlled by sponsors - eliminating fraud and giving them full transparency and proof of ROI into their promotional campaigns.

Sku-Level Validated Payment

Offers can be tapped or scanned at the merchant in the same manner as they accept digital gift cards or credit card payments, but with the associated UPC(s) validated within the redemption process.

Incremental Revenue

We enable new sources of revenue generation for partners. From digital sampling and higher ticket item promotional offers to in-venue activations to online discounts - our solution provides valuable new ways for clients to immediately impact their top and bottom line.

Not a Discount

Offers are funded value towards the purchase of products and are accepted as a split tender transaction - i.e., Retailers can get paid the full product value at time of transaction, minimizing top-line revenue hits from discounting.

Trackable

Clients can track offers from delivery, fan mobile wallet download all the way through redemption online or at retail. The platform supplies rich data analytics giving Sponsors, Platforms, Teams, Venues and Influencers full traceability.
